My Bio


A collaborative, analytical and enthusiastic 'girl coder' and coffee expert with a passion for social impact. I have a data analytics certification from the University of Western Australia, where I learned how to use Python, SQL, Excel, and Tableau to analyze, visualize, and communicate data insights. Currently a student with She Codes Australia on the 2024/25 She Codes Plus cohort, with an aim to enter the realm of tech as a junior software developer.

Previously, I was the operations director at SEND Coffee, a London-based non-profit social enterprise that trains and employs young people with special educational needs and disabilities in the specialty coffee industry. I also ran my own coffee-centric training business, Homeground, and spent time in coffee origin countries to broaden my knowledge scope and learn from coffee producers directly.

Ultimately, my goal is to combine my passion for teaching, community and positive leadership with my new career in tech at like-minded organisations in Perth (and around the world).
